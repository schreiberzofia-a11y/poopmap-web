About Poop Map 🗺️💩

Poop Map is a user-driven platform designed to help people locate public restrooms wherever they are.

Whether you're traveling, spending time outdoors, or simply unfamiliar with your surroundings, finding a restroom can be important. Poop Map allows users to share information about nearby facilities — including parks, businesses, and temporary locations — to improve public access and awareness.

Purpose

Not every area has clearly marked or easy-to-find restrooms. Poop Map was created to provide a practical solution to this everyday problem by making reliable restroom information easily accessible.

What You Can Do

  • Search for restrooms near your current location
  • Add new restroom locations to the map
  • Leave ratings to inform others

How It Works

The platform relies on user contributions. Users simply add locations to the map.
